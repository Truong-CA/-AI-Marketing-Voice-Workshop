#### What is AI Marketing Voice?
AI Marketing Voice is a web application designed for small businesses and content creators. 

1. Users provide product information (name, description, price, target audience, platform, tone of voice, duration, CTA).
2. The application returns a structured marketing script (headline, hook, body content, CTA, hashtags) generated by **Amazon Bedrock**.
3. An audio file featuring a **Vietnamese** voiceover of that script (users can choose 1 out of 10 available voices) is generated by **VieNeu-TTS** running directly on the backend server, stored on **Amazon S3**, and delivered via **Amazon CloudFront**.

Additionally, when a user registers a new account, the system sends a notification email via **Amazon SES**. All critical activities (registration, login, script generation, audio creation, system errors) are recorded through **Amazon CloudWatch Logs** for seamless monitoring and debugging.
