## General Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment was friendly, welcoming, and highly collaborative. Members of the FCAJ community were always willing to provide support whenever I encountered difficulties, even outside regular working hours. The workspace was well organized and comfortable, allowing me to stay focused on my tasks. One possible improvement would be to organize more social activities or team-building events to strengthen relationships among team members.

### 2. Support from Mentors and Team Administrators

My mentors provided clear and detailed guidance throughout the internship. Whenever I encountered challenges, they patiently explained concepts and encouraged me to ask questions. The team administrators also offered timely assistance with documentation, administrative procedures, and other resources needed during the internship. I especially appreciated that my mentors encouraged me to explore solutions independently rather than simply providing direct answers, which greatly enhanced my problem-solving skills.

### 3. Relevance to My Academic Major

The tasks assigned during the internship were closely aligned with the knowledge I had learned at university while also introducing me to new technologies and concepts that were not covered in my coursework. This allowed me to reinforce my academic foundation while gaining valuable practical experience.

### 4. Learning and Professional Development

Throughout the internship, I acquired many new skills, including project management practices, teamwork, and professional communication in a corporate environment. My mentors also shared valuable industry experience and career advice, helping me gain a clearer understanding of future career opportunities.

### 5. Company Culture and Teamwork

The company fostered a positive and supportive working culture where everyone respected one another and worked together professionally while maintaining a friendly atmosphere. During periods of high workload, team members actively collaborated and supported each other regardless of their roles. This made me feel like a valued member of the team despite being an intern.

### 6. Internship Policies and Benefits

The company provided an internship allowance and offered flexible working arrangements whenever necessary. In addition, having the opportunity to participate in internal technical training sessions was one of the most valuable aspects of the internship experience.
